Skin Abnormality Prediction

The purpose of this tool is to classify skin abnormalies from seven different catergories: Actinic keratoses and intraepithelial carcinoma / Bowen's disease (akiec), basal cell carcinoma (bcc), benign keratosis-like lesions (solar lentigines / seborrheic keratoses and lichen-planus like keratoses, bkl), dermatofibroma (df), melanoma (mel), melanocytic nevi (nv) and vascular lesions (vasc). The model was trained and tested using the HAM10000 dataset, a large collection of multi-source dermatoscopic images of common pigmented skin lesions. The model has a train accuracy of ~72% and a test accuracy of ~70%.
